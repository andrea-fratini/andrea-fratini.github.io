I am a PhD student in Economics at Stapienza University of Rome supervised by Valeria Patella.
My research intrests span models of Sudden Stops, non rational expectations, Markov-Switching models and Computational Methods.

Currently, I am working on introducing distorted beliefs in models of Sudden Stops by means of an endogenous Markov-Switching mechanism.

Projects and past works

  • globalSolve: An R library to solve economic models by global methods
  • Master's thesis: "Endogenous Regime-Switching representation of Ambiguity Attitudes in a model of Sudden Stops"
